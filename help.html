Help & Support

How do I create an account?

Click on "Sign Up" in the top menu and fill in the required details. Once submitted, you’ll be registered and can start shopping immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, online banking, and e-wallets such as GrabPay and Touch 'n Go.

How can I track my order?

After your purchase, you’ll receive an email with a tracking link. You can also check your order status in your account under "My Orders".

Can I return an item?

Yes, items can be returned within 14 days of delivery. Please ensure the item is unused and in its original packaging.

Need more help?

Email us at support@ÉVOQUE.com or call us at +60 1-800-88-1234. Our team is available 9 AM – 6 PM, Monday to Friday.
